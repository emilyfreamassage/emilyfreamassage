About Emily Frea

Your trusted massage therapist with a passion for healing and wellness

Your Trusted Massage Therapist

With over 8 years of experience in therapeutic massage, I founded Emily Frea Massage to create a sanctuary where clients can escape the stresses of daily life and focus on their wellbeing.

My philosophy centers on the belief that massage therapy should be accessible, personalized, and truly transformative. Each session is tailored to your specific needs, whether you're seeking relief from chronic pain, recovery from athletic activity, or simply a moment of peace in your busy life.

My Mission

"To provide personalized, therapeutic massage experiences that promote healing, reduce stress, and enhance overall wellbeing in a serene and welcoming environment."

My Approach to Massage

I believe in a holistic approach to massage therapy that addresses not just physical tension but also mental and emotional stress. Each session begins with a thorough consultation to understand your specific needs and goals.

My techniques blend traditional Swedish massage with specialized modalities to create a customized experience. I'm committed to ongoing education to stay current with the latest research and techniques in the field of massage therapy.
